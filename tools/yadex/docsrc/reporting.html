Reporting bugs




If you find a bug, tell the list about it. It's important. Especially if it makes Yadex crash or lose data. However, there are a few things to bear in mind :

  • Some bugs are already known (cf. TODO). You don't need to spend time reporting them because I'm already aware of them.

  • A vague report like "it has crashed" is nearly useless. If you want me to fix the bug, you need to be specific. What's your hardware and software configuration ? What version of Yadex do you run ? What iwad and pwads do you use ? What exactly did you do ? What message did you get exactly ? I know that collecting all that information can be time consuming but there's no way around it. Consider including the output of "make showconf".

  • Some bugs don't come from programming errors but from flaws in the design. So they can't be fixed, unless I redesign and rewrite a significant portion of the program.
