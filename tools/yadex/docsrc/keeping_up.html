Keeping up to date

How you can know when a new version comes out without having to watch the homepage.



In spite of my efforts, new versions of Yadex tend to pop up every now and then. But don't be afraid, I'm vigilant. As soon as I see one coming, I raise the alarm on the yadex-announce mailing list. You should have enough time to run for cover.

I'm also likely to post an announcement on rec.games.computer.doom.editing and Freshmeat. Several news sites such as Doom center, Doomworld, Linux Game Tome, New Doom and Linuxgames have been witnessed to echo the announcements.

To download the latest version, see the Yadex home page.

To know what's cooking (that is if anything at all is cooking), subscribe to the yadex mailing list.
